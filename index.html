Building

Rooted in the American heartland, our team of makers cares about getting to know client needs and designing and crafting a better, responsive web for both businesses and users. We believe in doing things the right way—and that building right requires close teamwork with our clients. Join us?

Teaching

Driven by a deep conviction to see the web liberated from device constraints, Sparkbox leads the way toward a greater standard as we consult and educate on how to design and build responsive websites and web applications at conferences and for internal teams through our Build Right workshops.

Why We Work

The way we build reflects what we believe, who we wish to be, and why we get up in the morning.

  • Build Right

    Well-crafted work is valuable work. It embraces details, intentionality, and standards.

  • Solve Problems

    Asking right questions of real problems drives work that produces true results.

  • Honor Content

    The web exists to communicate message and provide function—these are our primary interests.

  • Design for Clarity

    Design should complement message and never obstruct users from it.

  • Embrace All Devices

    The measure of a site should be its quality of build, not its dimensions.

What You Get

  • Honesty and Experience

    Our goal is to make your project successful. We've been doing this for a long time, and we’ll say what needs to be said to guide you down a fruitful path. We welcome the same honesty from you when solving problems together.

  • New, Iterative Processes

    Tackling smaller chunks of a larger solution leads to better projects. Working effectively and efficiently can also lead to unique processes. We'll help you understand why we do what we do, and we think you'll enjoy the fresh outlook.

  • Insightful Initial Engagements

    Optional small starter projects let us produce powerful planning, scope, and estimates to solve problems. Our clients are free to implement that elsewhere. If they continue working with us, we're able to hit the ground going.

  • Collaborative Hourly Work

    Working hourly allows us to be flexible to changing needs and creates more collaborative projects. Our clients are happier with the results, and we're glad to be paid only for the work we've done.
